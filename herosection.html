I'm Keosha Sanderson

A UX & UI designer based in South Africa

I instruct software to look good by creating buttons, icons, and layouts that are easy to use by choosing colours, fonts, and images to make things visually appealing. I focus heavily on how the software feels and works for users by making sure that the use of the software is smooth and enjoyable, as well as deciding how buttons behave and how menus flow. In a nutshell, I create digital experiences that are both visually pleasing and user-friendly.
